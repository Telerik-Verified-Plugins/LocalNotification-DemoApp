LocalNotification Plugin Demo

This plugin enables an application to inform its users that it has something for them by triggering a notification. They are scheduled by your app and delivered on the same device.

A notification can contain a message, title, badge, sound and data.

Custom plugins don't work in the simulator and the AppBuilder companion app, so inside Telerik AppBuilder choose Run - Build - iOS/Android - App package.

Local Notifications

All of these trigger 10 seconds after you click them, so you have time to close the app if you like.
